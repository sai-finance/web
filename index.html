What's Included in Your Credit Report?

Transunion CIBIL Report typically includes:

  • Your latest CIBIL Score (300-900).
  • Summary of credit payment history from lenders.
  • Personal info (name, DOB, gender) linked to accounts.
  • List of loan/credit card application enquiries (last 36 months).
  • Details of active and inactive loan/credit card accounts.

Experian Report typically includes:

  • Your Experian credit score (300-900).
  • Summary of credit accounts with detailed payment history.
  • Personal information linked to your credit profile.
  • List of your applications for loans and credit cards.
  • (And potentially other credit-related details)

Frequently Asked Questions

What service does Sai Finance provide?

Sai Finance acts as a financial mediator, connecting borrowers seeking personal, home, business, or property loans with suitable banks and financial institutions in Venkateswarapuram. We provide guidance and support throughout the application process.

What is the minimum salary for a Personal Loan?

A minimum salary of ₹15,000 per month (credited to a bank account) is generally required. Cash salaries are not accepted.

What CIBIL score is needed?

A CIBIL score of 700 or higher is generally preferred or required by most lenders we partner with. You can check your official score for free here: Check Free CIBIL Score.

How do I apply for a loan through Sai Finance?

You can start by clicking the "Apply Online" button which links to our application form: Online Application Form. Alternatively, use the Founder Contact Form or call us directly.

What documents are typically required?

Basic documents usually include PAN Card, Aadhaar Card (or other ID/Address proof), Bank Statements (6-12 months), Photos, and income proof like Salary Slips (for salaried) or ITR/Financials (for self-employed). See the Eligibility & Documents section for a general overview, or check this detailed list: Detailed Document Checklist.

Are there any fees charged by Sai Finance?

Yes, as a mediator providing facilitation services, Sai Finance charges a commission (Mediator Fee). This is separate from any processing fees or other charges levied by the actual lending institution. All fees are disclosed upfront for transparency.

What are the typical customer charges or expenses involved in getting a loan?

Besides the lender's interest rate, customers may incur several charges depending on the loan type and lender. These can include Login/Application Fees, Processing Fees (typically 1%-2.5% + GST), Legal/Notary Fees, Property-related charges like EC/MOD (for HL/LAP), and potentially Insurance costs. Sai Finance also charges a separate Mediator Fee for our services. For a detailed illustrative breakdown of potential expenses, please refer to this document: Detailed Customer Expenses Overview.
