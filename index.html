Eligibility & Documents

Common Eligibility Criteria

  • Personal Loans: Minimum Salary ₹15,000 (Cash Salary Not Accepted).
  • CIBIL Score: Preferred score above 700.
  • Business Loans: For small businesses with at least 2 years of operation. Turnover requirements vary.

Required Documents Overview

Specific documents depend on the loan type and employment status, but generally include:

  • Identity Proof (PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Voter's ID, etc.)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Rent Agreement, etc.)
  • Income Proof (Salary Slips, Bank Statements, IT Returns, Form 16)
  • Passport Size Photo(s)
  • Business Proof (for self-employed)
