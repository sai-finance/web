Frequently Asked Questions

What service does Sai Finance provide?

Sai Finance acts as a financial mediator, connecting borrowers seeking personal, home, business, or property loans with suitable banks and financial institutions in Venkateswarapuram. We provide guidance and support throughout the application process.

What is the minimum salary for a Personal Loan?

A minimum salary of ₹15,000 per month (credited to a bank account) is generally required. Cash salaries are not accepted.

What CIBIL score is needed?

A CIBIL score of 700 or higher is generally preferred or required by most lenders we partner with.

How do I apply for a loan through Sai Finance?

You can start by clicking the "Apply Online" button which links to our application form: Online Application Form. Alternatively, use the "Quick Contact" form or call us directly.

What documents are typically required?

Basic documents usually include PAN Card, Aadhaar Card (or other ID/Address proof), Bank Statements (6-12 months), Photos, and income proof like Salary Slips (for salaried) or ITR/Financials (for self-employed). See the Eligibility & Documents section for a general overview.

Are there any fees charged by Sai Finance?

Yes, as a mediator providing facilitation services, Sai Finance charges a commission (Mediator Fee) of ₹30,000. This is separate from any processing fees or other charges levied by the actual lending institution. All fees are disclosed upfront for transparency.
